## Captured LI

Added a classroom notes vocabulary site goal and prompt.

The LI defines:

- a static GitHub Pages-compatible site;
- tabs for Nowns, Verbs, and Other;
- vocabulary maintained in site data;
- real selectable Italian text;
- visual cues beside vocabulary;
- optional English visibility;
- browser speech synthesis with `it-IT`;
- best-available Italian voice selection;
- learner override via voice picker.
