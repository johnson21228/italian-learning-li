# Greeting Response Loop

## Purpose

Practice greetings and responses as a small social exchange.

The goal is not to memorize a list. The goal is to hear a greeting, respond, and stay in conversation.

## Loop

```text
Hear → Imitate → Answer → Repair → Capture
```

## Starter exchange

```text
A: Ciao.
B: Ciao.
A: Come stai?
B: Sto bene.
A: A presto.
B: A presto.
```

## Response choices

Use one short answer at a time:

```text
Molto bene.
Sto bene.
Bene.
Abbastanza bene.
Non c'è male.
Così così.
Male.
```

## Practice prompts

1. Hear: `Come stai?`
2. Imitate: say `Come stai?` aloud.
3. Answer: choose one response, such as `Sto bene.`
4. Repair: say `Puoi ripetere lentamente?` if needed.
5. Capture: record the phrase and situation in the personal corpus.

## Constraint

Do not begin by explaining `stare`. First use `Come stai?` and `Sto bene.` in a real exchange.
