# Formal / Informal Choice Loop

## Purpose

Help the learner choose between informal and formal Italian in first contact.

## First distinction

```text
Ciao. Come stai?       → informal
Buona sera. Come sta?  → formal
```

## Tu / Lei / voi

```text
Tu  = one person, singular, informal
Lei = one person, singular, formal
Voi = two or more people, plural, formal or informal
```

## Loop

1. Identify the situation: friend, stranger, teacher, older adult, group.
2. Choose `tu`, `Lei`, or `voi` posture.
3. Speak the smallest appropriate greeting.
4. Listen for the response.
5. Capture the phrase pair in the corpus.

## Examples

### Informal

```text
A: Ciao.
B: Ciao.
A: Come stai?
B: Sto bene.
```

### Formal

```text
A: Buona sera.
B: Buona sera.
A: Come sta?
B: Sto bene, grazie.
```

## Constraint

Do not turn this into a table-only lesson. The learner must speak both versions aloud and feel the social difference.
