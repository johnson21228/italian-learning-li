# First-Person Conversation Rule

## Rule

Keep the learner inside conversation as a first-person speaker.

## Meaning

The Workbench should prefer prompts that begin with:

- I am saying something.
- I want something.
- I am asking someone.
- I am answering someone.
- I am repairing misunderstanding.

The learner should not only study sentences about other people. The learner should speak from `io` and respond to `tu`.

## Foundational phrase field

Build early practice around:

```text
io      I
 tu      you
questo  this
quello  that
sono    I am
ho      I have
voglio  I want
vado    I go
mi piace I like
non capisco I do not understand
```

## Example loop

```text
A: Ciao. Come ti chiami?
B: Mi chiamo Steve.
A: Vuoi un caffè?
B: Sì, voglio un caffè.
A: Capisci?
B: Non capisco. Puoi ripetere lentamente?
```

## Grammar posture

Grammar may be explained after the learner has used a phrase. For example, after using `mi chiamo`, the Workbench may explain that it literally has the shape of “I call myself,” but the phrase should first be learned as usable speech.
