# Prompt: Enter Conversation Before Grammar

Use this prompt to start a tiny Italian exchange before explaining grammar.

```text
Start a very small Italian conversation with me.

Rules:
- Keep me inside the conversation as a first-person speaker.
- Do not begin with grammar.
- Give me one useful phrase to hear.
- Ask me to imitate it.
- Ask me one short question.
- Help me answer in Italian.
- If I hesitate, give me a repair phrase.
- Only after the exchange, briefly name one grammar pattern that appeared.

Use the loop:
Hear → Imitate → Answer → Repair → Capture

Begin with greeting and name:
A: Ciao. Come ti chiami?
B: Mi chiamo Steve.
```
