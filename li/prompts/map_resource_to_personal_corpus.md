# Prompt — Map Uploaded Resource to Personal Corpus

Use the Italian Learning LI to turn an uploaded resource section into personal corpus material.

Read first:

```text
source/resources/italian_learning_resource_index.md
li/source/uploaded_resource_custody_rule.md
li/prompts/capture_personal_corpus_entry.md
```

Task:

Take a short section, phrase family, or page range from one uploaded Italian resource and convert it into personal corpus entries.

For each entry capture:

```text
resource
section/page if known
phrase heard
phrase spoken
situation
meaning
pronunciation note
repair needed
next phrase wanted
```

Boundary:

Do not copy long passages from copyrighted resources. Summarize and transform into learner-owned practice.

Prioritize first-person speech and practical need.
