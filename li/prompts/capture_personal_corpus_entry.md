# Prompt: Capture Personal Corpus Entry

Use this prompt after a small exchange.

```text
Create a personal corpus entry from this Italian practice.

Capture:
- phrase heard
- phrase spoken
- situation
- meaning
- pronunciation note
- repair needed
- next phrase wanted

Keep the entry simple and reusable.
Do not over-explain grammar unless I ask.
```

## Corpus entry shape

```text
Phrase heard:
Phrase spoken:
Situation:
Meaning:
Pronunciation note:
Repair needed:
Next phrase wanted:
```
