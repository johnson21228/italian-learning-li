# Card 001 — Start Italian Learning Workbench

## Intent

Create a minimal Italian Learning Intelligence Workbench from the Workbench template pattern.

## Accepted direction

The Workbench begins from the human drive to communicate, not from grammar-first instruction.

The learner should be treated as a first-person speaker trying to participate in conversation.

## First principle

Italian learning begins with immediate communicative purposes:

- greeting;
- naming;
- wanting;
- needing;
- asking;
- answering;
- noticing;
- repairing misunderstanding.

## Corpus outlook

The Workbench should grow a personal corpus of encountered Italian:

- phrases heard;
- phrases spoken;
- phrases needed;
- phrases misunderstood;
- phrases repaired;
- pronunciation notes;
- situations of use.
