## What this resource provides

This resource contributes immediate survival/travel speech.

The table of contents and track list show these useful areas:

- Italian pronunciation;
- greetings and goodbyes;
- etiquette and social niceties;
- introductions and self-identification;
- numbers;
- compliments, likes, and dislikes;
- ordering food and drink;
- getting places in a taxi;
- hotel needs;
- communication strategies;
- shopping;
- emergencies;
- telephone etiquette;
- common first and last names;
- food glossary and menu reader;
- dates and holidays;
- street signs and useful signs;
- basic geography and cultural facts.
