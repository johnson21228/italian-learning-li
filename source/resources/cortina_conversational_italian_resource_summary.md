## Title and source identity

`Conversational Italian in 20 Lessons` is a Cortina Method book. The uploaded scan presents it as an illustrated self-study course with a simplified system of phonetic pronunciation, based on the method of R. Diez de la Cortina and authored by Michael Cagno.
