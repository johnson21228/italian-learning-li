# How LI Rules Work Here

Language Infrastructure files are durable guidance for future reasoning.

A good LI file in this Workbench should:

- state one clear learning rule;
- explain why it matters for Italian acquisition;
- give small examples of use;
- avoid overbuilding a curriculum too early;
- keep the learner in first-person communicative situations.

Prompts are reusable learning moves. Cards are continuity records for accepted Workbench direction.
